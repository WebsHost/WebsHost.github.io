Mall of the Emirates

Article Written By: Andrew ffrench, 10F

The Mall of The Emirates isn't the biggest mall, but don't let that get your hopes down - it's still MASSIVE! Boasting an entire indoor ski slope called "Ski Dubai", that is the first snow park and ski resort in the Middle East! It also contains a 14 screen VOX multiplex, a Magic Planet family entertainment area, the largest carrefour in Dubai and much, much more...
