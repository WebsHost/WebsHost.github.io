Dubai Mall

Dubai Mall is the biggest shopping mall in the world by total area, if you're a shopaholic you'll find Dubai Mall to be shopping heaven! The Dubai Mall is located at the foot of the Burj Khalifa and stretches a total of 1.1 million square meters! It has something for everyone! There is entertainment, leisure, shopping, eating, and so much more! With over 1,200 retail outlets, waterfalls, the Dubai Aquarium, theme parks (yes, inside a mall) and its own ice rink (again yes, inside a mall!). It's impossible to have nothing to do!

Mall of The Emirates

The Mall of The Emirates isn't the biggest mall, but don't let that get your hopes down - it's still MASSIVE! Boasting an entire indoor ski slope called "Ski Dubai", that is the first snow park and ski resort in the Middle East! It also contains a 14 screen VOX multiplex, a Magic Planet family entertainment area, the largest carrefour in Dubai and much, much more...

Indian Restaurant

Dubai has a rich history thanks to the large number immigrants, thanks to this Dubai has a range of great and authentic Indian Restaurants to choose from. Take your pick from a variety of Indian restaurants all throughout Dubai, there may even be a few right next to you!

Italian Restaurant

When Italian immigrants first came to Dubai it was said that they were outraged that there was no authentic Italian pizza and pasta, luckily for you, by now there are hundreds scattered all around Dubai! If you know where to look you can get the best pizza you've ever tasted (it will take you right back to Italy!)... or you can just find them using our services!
