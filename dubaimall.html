Dubai Mall

Article Written By: Andrew ffrench, 10F

Dubai Mall is the biggest shopping mall in the world by total area, if you're a shopaholic you'll find Dubai Mall to be shopping heaven! The Dubai Mall is located at the foot of the Burj Khalifa and stretches a total of 1.1 million square meters! It has something for everyone! There is entertainment, leisure, shopping, eating, and so much more! With over 1,200 retail outlets, waterfalls, the Dubai Aquarium, theme parks (yes, inside a mall) and its own ice rink (again yes, inside a mall!). It's impossible to have nothing to do!
